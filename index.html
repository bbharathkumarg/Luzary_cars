Maruti Suzuki Fronx

Primarily based on the Baleno premium hatchback, the Fronx has succeeded for Maruti in India. Sold via Nexa outlets, it is available with three engines and two gearbox options. Although crash test ratings are awaited and might be average at best, the Fronx scores high marks for space and comfort. It is a value-based offering for the masses, and for enthusiasts, Maruti offers a 1000cc turbo petrol option. We love how it looks, and this is a recommended alternative to the regular sub 4m compact SUVs.

₹8.10 - ₹14.84 Lakh*

Top Speed: 305 km/h
